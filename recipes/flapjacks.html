Flapjacks

Description

Flapjacks are a declicous combination of butter, golden syrup, and oats cooked in a flat oven tin and cut into squares or rectangles. They are great as little snacks to take on the go with you and like scones, are also relatively simple to make. Recipe from BBC's goodfood Yummy golden syrup flapjacks.

Ingredients

  • 250g jumbo porridge oats
  • 125g butter, plus extra for the tin
  • 125g light brown sugar
  • 2-3 tbsp golden syrup (depending on how gooey you want it)

Steps

  1. heat the oven to 200c/180c fan/gas 6.
  2. Put the oats, butter, sugar and golden syrup in blender.
  3. Pulse the blender until mixed, be careful not to overmix or the oats may lose their texture
  4. Lightly butter a 20x20cm baking tin and add the mixture.
  5. Press into the corners with the back of a spoon so the mixture is flat.
  6. Score into 12 squares.
  7. Bake for around 15 mis until golden brown.
  8. Enjoy!
