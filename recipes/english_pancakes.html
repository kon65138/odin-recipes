English pancakes

Description

Pancakes are flat, light, cakes that are cooked in a frying pan and typically made of eggs milk and flour. This recipe is based on the BBC's goodfood Easy pancakes recipe but I have added my own tiny twists to it. Pancakes are made with simple ingredients and are a fun and easy treat to make for yourself.

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 300ml milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1tbsp sunflower of vegetable oil. plus a little extra for frying
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1tbsp brown sugar

Steps

  1. Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk, 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug, then whisk to a smooth batter.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of groud cinnamon and 1 tablespoon of brown sugar to the mix.
  3. set a frying pan on medium heat and put oil on it, just enough so the pancakes are lubricated. (not alot)
  4. When hot, cook your pancakes for roughly 1 min on each side, the goal is for them to be golden brown on each side but not black/burnt.
  5. Serve with a sweet or sour topping or on their own, the choice is yours.
